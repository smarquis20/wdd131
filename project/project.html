Recipe Box

Recipe Box is a user-friendly platform designed to help users organize, store, and access their favorite recipes in one convenient place. Users can easily add new recipes, categorize them by cuisine, meal type, or ingredients, and tag them with personal notes or dietary preferences. The website offers a search feature, allowing users to quickly locate recipes based on keywords or ingredients on hand.
